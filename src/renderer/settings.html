Wake Word
Always-on listening — say the phrase to activate Friday hands-free
Off

Requires Whisper to be configured. The phrase is matched case-insensitively anywhere in the transcription. Any words after the phrase are sent as a command automatically.

How it works:
Click the 🎤 mic button in the chat input, speak, then click it again to stop. Whisper transcribes the recording locally using your GPU (CUDA) — typically under 1 second for short clips on an RTX-class card.

Falls back to OS speech recognition if Whisper paths are not configured.

How to use
1. Rate responses with 👍 during normal chat to build your dataset.
2. Export here when you have enough examples (50+ recommended).
3. Fine-tune with llama.cpp:
./finetune --model base.gguf --train-data friday-training.jsonl --lora-out lora.bin 4. Load the LoRA adapter in Ollama or llama.cpp server.
